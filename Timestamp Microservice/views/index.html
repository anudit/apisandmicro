Timestamp-Microservice


Timestamp Microservice for FreeCodeCamps API-Projects

How to use it:

You can use a natural language date matching one of the following formats:


.../2016-12-02
.../02-12-2016
.../December 2, 2016
.../December 2 2016
.../Dec 2, 2016
.../Dec 2 2016
.../2 December 2016
.../2 December 16
.../2 Dec 2016
.../2 Dec 16

Or a Unix-Timestamp:


.../1480636800

Output:


{
 unix:  1480636800,
 natural: "December 2, 2016"
}
